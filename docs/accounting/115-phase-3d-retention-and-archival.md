# Phase 3D retention and archival

Retention settings classify accounting records, bank statements, invoices, audit logs, attachments, and close packets in whole years. Settings are versioned and require a narrow management capability. Phase 3D does not automatically purge journal history and does not prescribe jurisdiction-specific legal periods.

Legal holds identify an entity and reason, record placement and release, and block archival while active. Archiving removes a closed fiscal year from ordinary current navigation without deleting or changing journals, sources, reconciliation evidence, audit events, snapshots, reports, or balances.
