# Phase 5B Privacy, Consent, and Safety

Skills & Service listings are self-reported. AGAPAY and the parish do not verify licenses, credentials, insurance, background checks, or suitability.

## Consent Rules

- Members may save drafts without publication.
- Activation records consent, source, policy version, and timestamp.
- Withdrawal removes ordinary member visibility and records withdrawal time.
- Staff cannot restore a withdrawn listing without valid member consent.
- Staff may hide or archive listings for parish safety and moderation.

## Publication Rules

- Ordinary members see only active, member-visible adult listings.
- Private and staff-only listings do not appear in member search.
- Staff-only parish mode disables ordinary member search for Skills & Service.
- Children are excluded.
- Protected people are excluded from ordinary member results.
- Contact display uses only contact methods that are already published to directory members, or falls back to the parish office message.

## Cache And Distribution

Member-facing and staff-facing private responses use no-store cache headers. Print and export endpoints are private parish dashboard functions and are audited when generated.

## Known Non-Goals

Phase 5B does not add public pages, booking, payments, direct messaging, calendar scheduling, location maps, license verification, background checks, recommendation ranking, or automated matching.
