Why AgaPay

Not adapted for Orthodoxy.
Built for it.

Tithe.ly and Pushpay were built for evangelical megachurches. They don't know what a panakhida is. They've never heard of the Typikon. AgaPay was built by someone who has stood in the nave, held a taper at Pascha, and submitted names for commemoration at the proskomedia.

Built by a Reader, for the Church

AgaPay was built by a tonsured Reader of the Russian Orthodox Church Outside Russia — a husband and father of ten, committed to classical Orthodox education for his family. I saw what was missing in how our parishes handle stewardship and giving, and I decided to build what the Church actually needs. Every feature was shaped by someone who knows this life from the inside. That is not something any competitor can replicate.
