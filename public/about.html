The story

A legacy beyond the courtroom.

As an attorney, I've spent well over a decade seeking justice. I have spent thousands of hours in courtrooms and at desks, arguing cases and serving the interests of justice. It is honorable work. But I also realize that this work will not outlive me.

As Holy Orthodoxy continues to grow exponentially in the American land, there is an increasing need for its congregants to start building the infrastructure that the Church uniquely requires. If we are to cement Orthodoxy into the American land, we all share the vital, active role in the growth and maturation of Orthodoxy here in America.

That is why I built AgaPay.

"I want to create something that carries the faith forward and makes a meaningful contribution to the missionary work of spreading the Ancient Christian faith in the West."

The need

Love how you give.

When I surveyed the giving platforms used by our parishes — tools like Tithe.ly or Pushpay — I saw software engineered for a completely different theology. Western Christianity has lost the fronema of the Ancient Church, its liturgical cycle, and a deep understanding of the virtue of charity.

As a tonsured Reader, I know what it means to stand in the nave at Pascha and to submit names for commemoration at the proskomedia. AgaPay is built from the inside out. Every line of code, every feature, and every design choice reflects the lived experience of the Orthodox Tradition. That is our foundation, and it is a distinction no competitor can replicate.

The vision

Modern infrastructure for the ancient act of Christian charity.

Holy Orthodoxy is still putting down roots in the American land. The missions being planted, the monasteries being built, and the children being raised in the faith today are the bedrock of what will endure for centuries if we tend it faithfully.

AgaPay is my contribution to that foundation. It is the giving platform designed to make our parishes financially resilient, enabling the faithful to give with the same intentionality they bring to the chalice.

Orthodox Christians should be able to give on a platform that shares not only their values, but also their way of life.

Rooted in Tradition

We don't force the Church to adapt to heterodox giving platforms. We build AgaPay to serve the liturgical life of the Church.

Honest stewardship

Clear fees, honorable practices, and an open business model — in service of the building and growth of Orthodox parishes and monasteries.

One Body, One Platform

AgaPay is built to serve the Body of Christ — honoring the oldest model of faithful, purposeful charity and almsgiving.

"Do almsgiving based on the quantity of your possessions. If you possess only a few, do not be afraid to give according to the little you have. You are storing up a good treasure for yourself in the day of necessity. For almsgiving delivers us from death and prevents us from entering into the darkness. Indeed, almsgiving is a good gift for all who do it before the Most High."

— Tobit 4:8–11
