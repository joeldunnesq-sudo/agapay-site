Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 21, 2026

Legal Disclaimer: This Privacy Policy is provided for informational purposes. This document requires review by counsel to ensure compliance with state statutes (e.g., Texas Data Privacy and Security Act, CCPA/CPRA) and your specific data architecture.

1. Definitions & Roles

  • The Parish/Monastery ("Data Controller"): Acts as the Data Controller.
  • AgaPay ("Data Processor"): We process personal data solely on behalf of the Data Controller for the purpose of stewardship and donation management.

2. Information We Collect

  • Volunteered Information: Name, contact information, and liturgical intention/memorial lists.
  • Financial Data: Processed via Stripe. We do not store full credit card or banking credentials.
  • Technical Usage Data: IP address, device type, browser metadata.

3. Data Usage & Purpose

We process data to fulfill contractual obligations:

  • Gift processing and record-keeping.
  • Liturgical fulfillment (transmitting memorial names to clergy).
  • Tax compliance and charitable reporting.

4. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the CCPA/CPRA provides specific rights:

  • Notice at Collection: We collect the categories described in Section 2. We do not use this data for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Right to Know/Access/Delete/Correct: You have the right to request access, deletion, or correction of your personal data.
  • "Do Not Sell or Share": AgaPay does not "sell" or "share" personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA.

To exercise your rights, please submit a request to hello@agapay.app. We will verify your identity before processing.

5. Security & Retention

We employ administrative, technical, and physical safeguards, including AES-256 encryption. We retain data only for as long as necessary to perform services or comply with accounting laws.

6. Dispute Resolution & Arbitration

Any dispute shall be settled by binding arbitration in Lubbock, Texas. You and AgaPay agree that each may bring claims only in your or its individual capacity, and not as a plaintiff or class member in any class proceeding.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions, please contact us at: hello@agapay.app
