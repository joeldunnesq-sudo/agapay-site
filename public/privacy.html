Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 21, 2026

Legal Disclaimer: This Privacy Policy is provided for informational purposes as a draft template. This document requires review and customization by counsel to ensure compliance with specific state statutes (e.g., Texas Data Privacy and Security Act, CCPA/CPRA) and your specific platform data architecture.

1. Definitions & Roles

  • The Parish/Monastery ("Data Controller"): Acts as the Data Controller.
  • AgaPay ("Data Processor"): We process personal data solely on behalf of the Data Controller for the purpose of stewardship and donation management.

2. Information We Collect

  • Volunteered Information: Name, contact information, and liturgical intention/memorial lists.
  • Financial Data: Processed via Stripe. We do not store full credit card or banking credentials.
  • Technical Usage Data: IP address, device type, browser metadata.

3. Data Usage & Purpose

We process data to fulfill contractual obligations:

  • Gift processing and record-keeping.
  • Liturgical fulfillment (transmitting memorial names to clergy).
  • Tax compliance and charitable reporting.

4. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) provide you with specific rights regarding your personal information.

  • Notice at Collection: We collect the categories of personal information described in Section 2. We do not use this data for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Right to Know/Access: You have the right to request that we disclose what personal information we collect, use, and disclose.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request the deletion of personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions (e.g., tax record retention).
  • Right to Correct: You have the right to request the correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA/CPRA rights.
  • "Do Not Sell or Share": AgaPay does not "sell" or "share" (for cross-context behavioral advertising) your personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA.

To exercise your rights, please submit a request to hello@agapay.app. We will verify your identity before processing your request.

5. Security & Retention

We employ administrative, technical, and physical safeguards, including AES-256 encryption. We retain data only for as long as necessary to perform the services requested or to comply with applicable tax and accounting retention mandates.

6. Dispute Resolution & Arbitration

Any dispute arising out of this Privacy Policy shall be settled by binding arbitration administered by the American Arbitration Association (AAA) in Lubbock, Texas. You and AgaPay agree that each may bring claims against the other only in your or its individual capacity, and not as a plaintiff or class member in any purported class or representative proceeding.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions, please contact us at: hello@agapay.app
