AgaPay believes you should know exactly where every dollar goes. Here is the complete fee structure for every transaction — nothing omitted, nothing obscured.

How fees work on a $100 donation

Example: a parishioner gives $100 to their parish. Here is where every dollar goes.

Fee Who charges it Amount Notes
Payment processing Stripe $3.20 2.9% + $0.30 — industry standard
AgaPay platform fee AgaPay $0.50 0.5% for Parish tier — sustains the platform
Parish receives — $96.30 96.3% of every dollar given reaches the parish

Stripe is the payment processor that handles the actual movement of money — card processing, bank transfers, fraud protection, and compliance. It is the same infrastructure used by Amazon, Google, and most major platforms. Stripe charges 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction regardless of which platform you use. AgaPay is transparent about this cost because we believe you deserve to know exactly where your money goes.
