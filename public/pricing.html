Traditional church processors separate their platform fees from core card processing network fees, creating complex merchant statements. AgaPay uses a single, clear, flat transaction rate for each tier. This completely eliminates hidden percentages, clearing-house markups, and unexpected bookkeeping line items for your treasurer.
