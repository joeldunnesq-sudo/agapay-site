Category 1

About AGAPAY Learn

What is AGAPAY Learn?

AGAPAY Learn is a homeschool planning and recordkeeping application for parents. It helps families organize children, subjects, lessons, books, attendance, grades, printable plans, reports, and household rhythms in one place.

Is AGAPAY Learn a curriculum?

AGAPAY Learn is primarily a planner and recordkeeping tool. It helps parents organize and document their homeschool, but parents remain responsible for choosing curriculum, teaching lessons, evaluating work, and complying with applicable homeschool requirements.

Is AGAPAY Learn only for Orthodox families?

AGAPAY Learn was built by an Orthodox family and includes optional Orthodox formation tools, Church calendar support, saints, readings, feast days, fasting notes, and family prayer rhythms. TEFA families can use the academic planning tools without being forced into those Orthodox features. The Church rhythm tools remain available for families who want them.

What ages or grades does it support?

AGAPAY Learn is designed for K–12 household planning. Parents can organize children by familiar grades or by flexible Forms, which allow siblings at similar stages to share some work while still keeping individual records.

Category 2

Odyssey / TEFA Status

AGAPAY Learn has been pre-approved for Odyssey. Final marketplace setup is still being completed.

Has AGAPAY Learn been approved for Odyssey?

AGAPAY Learn has been pre-approved for Odyssey. Final setup is still in progress, including required onboarding steps. Marketplace availability and purchase timing remain subject to Odyssey's final setup process.

Can families purchase AGAPAY Learn through Odyssey right now?

Not yet, unless the marketplace listing has already gone live. AGAPAY Learn has been pre-approved, but final onboarding must be completed before families can purchase through Odyssey. Once the listing is live, families will purchase through Odyssey and activate their AGAPAY account using their Odyssey purchase reference.

How will AGAPAY Learn work with Odyssey?

Once Odyssey setup is complete and AGAPAY Learn is available through Odyssey, families will purchase AGAPAY Learn through Odyssey, then activate access on AGAPAY by entering the Odyssey purchase reference on the activation page.

How much does the TEFA/Odyssey Edition cost?

The AGAPAY Learn TEFA/Odyssey Edition is $89 per family, per year. The family plan covers the household, not each child separately.

Why is the TEFA/Odyssey Edition priced separately?

The TEFA/Odyssey Edition includes TEFA-oriented setup, recordkeeping language, Odyssey activation handling, support, and parent-facing documentation designed for homeschool families using education funds.

What happens after I purchase on Odyssey?

After purchasing through Odyssey, go to /learn/odyssey/dashboard/activate, create or sign into your AGAPAY account, and enter your Odyssey purchase reference. Activation is immediate, and AGAPAY may manually reconcile purchase references against Odyssey records.

What if I already have an AGAPAY account?

Use your existing AGAPAY email and password during activation. Your AGAPAY Learn TEFA subscription will be added to the existing account.

Category 3

TEKS / Records

What does "TEKS-aligned" mean?

AGAPAY Learn helps parents organize subjects, lessons, books, attendance, grades, and records using categories that can support Texas Essential Knowledge and Skills planning. It does not automatically guarantee legal or academic compliance by itself. Parents remain responsible for selecting curriculum and maintaining their homeschool.

Does AGAPAY Learn report anything to the State of Texas?

No. AGAPAY Learn is a parent-controlled planning and recordkeeping tool. It does not submit homeschool records, grades, attendance, reports, or child information to the State of Texas.

Can AGAPAY Learn create reports or transcripts?

AGAPAY Learn includes records, printables, grades, attendance, and report-oriented tools. Transcript-ready recordkeeping and report tools are being built carefully so parents can maintain organized academic records.

Can I print my plans?

Yes. AGAPAY Learn includes a Print Center for household plans, weekly planners, monthly calendars, child sheets, and other PDF planning resources.

Category 4

Household Setup

Can I add multiple children?

Yes. The family plan is designed for the whole household and supports multiple children under one parent-managed account.

Do my children need their own accounts?

No. AGAPAY Learn is designed around one parent-controlled household account. Children do not need separate logins.

Can I organize by grade instead of Forms?

Yes. Families can use familiar grade labels or Forms. Forms are flexible learning groups that help siblings share some work when appropriate.

Can I change my setup later?

Yes. Parents can adjust children, subjects, books, planning structure, calendar preference, and household rhythm later.

Category 5

Student Data & Privacy

Because AGAPAY Learn helps parents organize homeschool records, we treat child and household information with care. The goal is simple: parents control the account, parents control the records, and student data is not sold.

Parents control the account No separate child logins — everything runs through one parent-managed household account.
Not sold, not used for ads Student and household data is never sold and is not used for advertising targeting.
Limited access Only the parent account holder and, when needed for support, AGAPAY administrators can access account data.
What child information does AGAPAY Learn store?

AGAPAY Learn may store child names or nicknames, ages, grades, Forms, subjects, books, lesson plans, attendance, grades, notes, reports, printable planning data, and household rhythm information. Families using the Odyssey activation flow may also have an Odyssey purchase reference stored with the parent account.

Does AGAPAY sell student data?

No. AGAPAY does not sell student data.

Does AGAPAY use student data for advertising?

No. AGAPAY Learn is a parent-directed homeschool planning tool. Student records are not used for advertising targeting.

Do children have accounts?

No. Children do not need separate AGAPAY accounts. The household is managed through the parent's account.

Can parents request deletion?

Yes. A parent may request help deleting or reviewing household data by contacting support@agapay.app.

Can parents export their records?

AGAPAY Learn is built around parent-accessible records and printable outputs. If you need help reviewing or exporting account information, contact support@agapay.app.

Who can see my homeschool data?

The parent account holder can access the household's Learn data. AGAPAY administrators may access limited account information only as needed for support, security, troubleshooting, activation, or system maintenance.

Does AGAPAY submit records to Odyssey or the government?

No. AGAPAY does not submit homeschool lesson records, grades, attendance, books, or child information to Odyssey or the government as part of normal app use. Odyssey purchase references may be manually reconciled for activation or payment verification.

Category 6

Support

What if activation does not work?

Email support@agapay.app with your AGAPAY account email and Odyssey purchase reference. Do not include sensitive personal information beyond what is needed for support.

What if I bought through Odyssey but used the wrong AGAPAY email?

Contact support at support@agapay.app. AGAPAY can help reconcile the account and purchase reference.

How do I get help?

Email support@agapay.app.
