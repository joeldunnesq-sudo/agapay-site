What's included

Everything your homeschool needs, in one place.

AGAPAY Learn – TEFA Edition is a full-year Texas homeschool planning software — not a one-page worksheet generator. Plan your terms, track your children's progress, manage your reading lists, and print what you need.

Weekly & Term Planner

Plan your academic year by term, then zoom into weekly lessons for each child. Drag-and-drop subjects, mark completions, and see the week at a glance.

Multi-Child Household

Manage every child from one household view. Shared subjects run together, individual tracks stay separate. Unlimited children per family plan.

Transcripts & Reports

AGAPAY Learn doubles as a TEFA student records tool — generate official-looking transcripts with grade computation from your completed terms. Export as PDF for portfolios, college prep, or record-keeping requirements.

Book & Reading Tracker

Build your family's living book library. Track read-alouds, individual reading, copywork sources, and audiobooks. Filter by subject, form, or status.

Printable Planners

Generate print-ready weekly planners, lesson sheets, chore charts, and month calendars. Download as PDF directly from the dashboard — no desktop software required.

Family Planning Hub

Meal planning, chore schedules, family events, and recipe management all in one view. Run your household alongside your school — not in a separate app.

Common questions

Frequently asked questions.

Is AGAPAY Learn actually TEFA-eligible?

Yes. AGAPAY Learn — TEFA Edition is a Texas homeschool planning software that has been pre-approved for Odyssey as an Educational Application, an approved TEFA expense category. Final Odyssey marketplace setup is still in progress. Once complete, you'll purchase it through your Odyssey digital wallet using your TEFA funds. After Odyssey purchase, activate your AGAPAY Learn account here.

Does it align to Texas TEKS standards?

Yes. AGAPAY Learn uses a Forms-based subject structure that maps Charlotte Mason methods to TEKS expectations at each grade level. You get a literature-rich education that meets Texas standards — without abandoning the method.

How many children can I add?

Unlimited. The $89/yr family plan covers your entire household — all children, all grades. Each child gets their own individual track, and shared subjects run together for the whole family.

What if I already have an AGAPAY account?

Perfect — use it. During activation, sign in with your existing AGAPAY email and password, then enter your Odyssey purchase reference. Your TEFA subscription will be added to your existing account.

Can I use this portal separately from My AGAPAY?

Yes. The TEFA portal at agapay.app/learn/odyssey/dashboard goes directly to your homeschool planner. You'll never see the AGAPAY Give or Marketplace products unless you choose to.

What happens when my TEFA funds renew next year?

You simply repurchase AGAPAY Learn on Odyssey for the new school year and enter your new purchase reference on the activate page. Your planning data, books, and children's records carry over.
