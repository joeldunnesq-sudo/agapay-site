What AGAPAY Learn does

Orthodox homeschool planning for real family life.

AGAPAY Learn is an Orthodox homeschool planning app for families and a product of AGAPAY, hosted publicly at agapay.app/learn. Families use AGAPAY Learn to organize lessons, terms, books, printables, reports, and liturgical calendar rhythms in one household planner.

Google Calendar Sync

Optional calendar connection, used only for your homeschool plan.

If you choose to connect Google Calendar, AGAPAY Learn asks your permission only to help create or sync homeschool planning events to your calendar. This may include lesson blocks, term dates, feast days, reminders, or household planning events that you choose to send to Google Calendar. Google Calendar access is requested solely to provide this optional calendar sync feature.

  • AGAPAY Learn does not sell Google user data.
  • AGAPAY Learn does not use Google Calendar data for advertising.
  • You can use AGAPAY Learn without connecting Google Calendar.
