What AGAPAY Learn does

Orthodox homeschool planning for real family life.

Plan Catechesis, saints, hymns, poetry, art, and other shared enrichment once for the whole family. Then organize core subjects by Form, so children working at a similar developmental stage can learn together without duplicating the same plan for every child.

Family-based

Learning everyone shares together, such as Church rhythms, Catechesis, read-alouds, hymns, saints, poetry, artists, and composers.

What is a Form?

A Form is simply a group of children learning at a similar stage. Instead of planning separately for every grade, you can teach shared history, science, literature, or geography together and adjust the work for each child.
