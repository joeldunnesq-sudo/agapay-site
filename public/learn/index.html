What AGAPAY Learn does

One peaceful place to shape the school year.

AGAPAY Learn is an Orthodox Christian homeschool planning application for parents. It brings household setup, lessons, books, Church rhythms, weekly progress, and beautiful printable plans into one thoughtful workspace.

Families can use familiar grade levels or group children at a similar developmental stage into Forms. Shared enrichment is planned once for the household, while core subjects and books are assigned where they belong.

01

Family-based

Learning everyone shares together, such as Church rhythms, Catechesis, read-alouds, hymns, saints, poetry, artists, and composers.

02

What is a Form?

A Form is simply a group of children learning at a similar stage. Instead of planning separately for every grade, you can teach shared history, science, literature, or geography together and adjust the work for each child.

Optional Google Calendar Sync

Your plan can travel with your family calendar.

When a signed-in parent chooses to connect Google Calendar, AGAPAY Learn requests only the Calendar events permission. It creates and updates selected lesson blocks, term dates, feast days, and reminders in the user's primary calendar, and checks previously synced AGAPAY Learn events to prevent duplicates. Calendar connection is optional; AGAPAY Learn works without it. AGAPAY Learn does not sell Google user data or use Google Calendar data for advertising. Users may disconnect or revoke access at any time.
