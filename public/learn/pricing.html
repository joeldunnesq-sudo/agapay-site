Questions families ask

Practical answers before you begin.

Why not charge per child?

Because Orthodox homes are not subscription units. AGAPAY Learn is priced by household so large families are not penalized for welcoming children.

What if we have a baby or a hard season?

Grace Mode helps you lighten the day, preserve the essentials, and keep a record of what actually happened.

Can I use my own curriculum?

Yes. AGAPAY Learn supports living books, custom curricula, and the materials your family already loves.

What can I print now?

The current Print Center supports practical household plans, child sheets, calendars, and planning pages. Reports, report cards, and transcripts are clearly marked as coming soon.
