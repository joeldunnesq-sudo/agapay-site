How AGAPAY Works

For the Faithful

Giving should feel like giving.

Support your parish, monastery, or mission in a few simple steps — with offerings, intentions, and commemorations shaped around Orthodox life.

Four simple steps

From first visit to first offering.

✝
1

Sign up with AGAPAY

Create your account in about a minute. No app to download, no setup fees — just your name and email to begin.

2

Find your parish

Search by name, city, jurisdiction, or location to find your parish, mission, or monastery — across every canonical jurisdiction.

3

Choose your offering

Select the way you would like to give — each shaped around the life of the Church:

Stewardship & recurring gifts Candles for the living and the departed Commemorations for the living and the departed Special appeals & building funds
4

Love how you give

Your gift supports real people and real mission in the Church.

✓Your parish is notified.
✓Your gift is delivered.
✓Your intentions are remembered — commemorations reach your priest each week.
✓You make a difference in the life of the Church.
