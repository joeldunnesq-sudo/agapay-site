Add AGAPAY to your phone

Your giving, a tap away.

Add our web-based app to your home screen for quick access to your giving, intentions, and commemorations. It opens straight from your browser — no app store, no download.

1

Tap Install AGAPAY below. Your browser will ask to add our web-based app to your home screen.

2

Confirm Install, and the AGAPAY icon appears with your other apps.

3

Open it anytime to give, light a candle, or submit names for commemoration.

If your browser doesn’t prompt automatically, it will open AGAPAY so you can choose “Add to Home screen” from the browser menu.

1

Open AGAPAY in Safari (iPhone and iPad can add our web-based app from Safari).

2

Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen.

3

Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen, then Add. The AGAPAY icon appears with your other apps.

On iPhone & iPad, use Safari’s Share menu — Apple doesn’t allow a one-tap install button.
