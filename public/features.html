Features

Every way the Church gives,
finally in one place.

AgaPay is built around three distinct giving modes — each shaped by how Orthodox Christians actually live and give, not by how a generic platform thinks they should.

The three giving tiers

Stewardship. Funds. Campaigns.

Most giving platforms treat all donations the same. AgaPay understands that a weekly tithe, a memorial offering, and a disaster relief campaign are fundamentally different acts — and treats them accordingly.

I

Regular Stewardship

The foundational tithe — recurring, faithful, tied to the rhythm of the liturgical year rather than arbitrary billing cycles.

  • Weekly and monthly recurring pledges
  • Annual stewardship campaigns
  • Liturgical calendar triggers — Great Lent, Pascha, Nativity
  • Automatic giving statements for tax purposes
  • Parishioner giving history and pledge tracking
II

Designated Funds

Named, purposeful, permanent. A fund for the new iconostasis is not the same as a fund for the general operating budget.

  • Building and renovation funds
  • Iconostasis and beautification campaigns
  • Seminary and education scholarships
  • Parish school endowments
  • Restricted fund tracking with transparency reporting
III

Campaign Giving

Acute, urgent, time-bound — with the canonical verification that GoFundMe can never offer.

  • Disaster and emergency relief appeals
  • Health crisis and medical funds
  • Love offerings for clergy and families in need
  • Time-limited feast day campaigns
  • Canonically verified — every campaign vetted
Who it serves

Parishes and monasteries, equally.

A parish and a monastery have different financial realities, different rhythms, and different relationships with giving. AgaPay treats them as equals — not as an afterthought.

For parishes

The full stewardship platform

Everything a parish treasurer, priest, and council needs to run a financially healthy community.

  • Weekly stewardship and recurring pledge management
  • Feast day and candle offering collection
  • Panakhida and parastas memorial donations with names submitted to the priest's queue
  • Designated building and capital campaign funds
For monasteries

Free, always — because it should be

Monasteries give everything to the Church. AgaPay gives back by charging them nothing.

  • Zero monthly platform fee — permanently free for all monasteries and sketes
  • Product and craft sale campaigns — prayer ropes, books, icon prints
  • Retreat and pilgrimage registration giving
  • General support and oblation campaigns
