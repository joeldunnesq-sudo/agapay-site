The Platform

Every way the Church gives.

Finally in one place.

AGAPAY is the purpose-built giving platform for Orthodox parishes and monasteries.

We unify stewardship, designated funds, and campaigns in a secure, beautiful platform that honors our faith and simplifies giving.

How your money actually moves

Donations go directly
to your church.

Most giving platforms collect your donation into their own account first — then disburse it to the church on their schedule. For a window of time, they hold your money. That introduces custody risk, disbursement delays, and regulatory complexity none of us want.

AGAPAY uses Stripe Connect's direct charge architecture — meaning your gift flows straight to the parish's or monastery's own Stripe account at the moment you give. AGAPAY collects its small platform fee at the same instant, as a pass-through. We never touch your principal.

This isn't a technical detail. It's a commitment: your stewardship belongs to the Church, not to a platform.

Three Ways the Church Gives

✝

Most giving platforms treat every donation the same. AGAPAY understands that a weekly tithe, a memorial offering, and a relief campaign are fundamentally different acts — and treats them accordingly.

Regular Stewardship

Faithful. Recurring. Foundational.
  • Weekly and monthly recurring pledges
  • Annual stewardship campaigns
  • Liturgical calendar timing — Great Lent, Pascha, Nativity
  • Automatic giving statements for tax purposes
  • Parishioner giving history and pledge tracking

Designated Funds

Purposeful. Transparent. Permanent.
  • Building and renovation funds
  • Iconostasis and beautification campaigns
  • Seminary and education scholarships
  • Parish school endowments
  • Restricted-fund tracking with transparent reporting

Campaign Giving

Urgent. Time-bound. Verified.
  • Disaster and emergency relief appeals
  • Health crisis and medical funds
  • Love offerings for clergy and families in need
  • Time-limited feast day campaigns
  • Canonically verified — every campaign vetted

From registration to first offering
in under an hour.

✝

Designed for parish councils, not software teams. If you can send an email, you can set up AGAPAY.

1

Register with AGAPAY

A priest or parish council administrator submits a registration request. We verify canonical standing before activation — ensuring only legitimate Orthodox communities use the platform.

2

Configure your parish profile

Set your jurisdiction, calendar (Julian or Gregorian), patronal feast, and offering types. ROCOR, GOA, Antiochian — every jurisdiction and language supported.

3

Connect your Stripe account

Your parish connects its own Stripe account — or creates one in minutes. From this moment, all donations flow directly to you. AGAPAY never holds your funds.

4

Share with your parishioners

Every parish receives a clean, dedicated giving page. Share it in your bulletin, from the ambo, or via email. No app download required for donors.

5

Names go to the priest automatically

Memorial and candle offerings are formatted and sent directly to the priest's commemoration queue — ready for the proskomedia. Nothing is lost, nothing overlooked.

6

Treasurer sees everything, clearly

Every offering is categorized, dated, and attributed. Exportable reports, annual giving statements — generated automatically at year end. Nothing to reconcile manually.
