Why AGAPAY

Why AGAPAY

Built for the nave. Not the megachurch.

Most giving platforms were built for large, corporate-style ministries, then adapted outward. AGAPAY started somewhere different - inside the Tradition, built by someone who knows the difference between a panikhida and a parastas.

What sets us apart

Things a general platform isn't built to do.

These aren't features added to court Orthodox parishes. They're the reason AGAPAY exists.

Built from within the Tradition

AGAPAY is being built by a tonsured Reader of ROCOR - not a fintech company entering the religious market. Every decision is shaped by someone who lives this life from the inside.

The liturgical calendar is native

Both Julian and Gregorian calendars are supported, and giving campaigns follow the festal cycle - Nativity, Theophany, Great Lent, Pascha, Soul Saturdays - rather than generic seasonal windows.

Commemoration lists reach the priest

When a parishioner submits a memorial offering, the names are formatted and routed toward the priest's proskomedia preparation - designed so they aren't lost in a spreadsheet.

Canonical standing is reviewed

Every parish and monastery is reviewed for canonical standing before activation, so a gift through AGAPAY reaches a recognized community.

Monasteries pay no monthly fee

Monasteries and sketes carry no monthly platform fee. This was decided at founding and isn't a promotion; standard transaction fees still apply, but the monthly subscription is free, permanently.

Month-to-month, no lock-ins

No annual contracts, no cancellation penalties, no multi-year commitments. A platform should earn a parish's trust each month.

Where we're transparent

AGAPAY's pricing is $49 / $99 per month plus 5% + $0.30 per transaction. General platforms may advertise a lower per-transaction card rate or a free base tier; we've chosen to fold platform value into a simple, predictable structure rather than gate features behind higher monthly plans.
