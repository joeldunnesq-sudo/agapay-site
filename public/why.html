Why AGAPAY

Why AGAPAY

Built for the nave. Not the megachurch.

Most giving platforms were built for large, corporate-style ministries, then adapted outward. AGAPAY started somewhere different - inside the Tradition, built by someone who knows the difference between a panikhida and a parastas.

What sets us apart

Built from inside Orthodox life.

AGAPAY was not adapted for the Church after the fact. It was built from within the Tradition — for the nave, the candle stand, the treasurer’s desk, the priest’s commemoration list, and the life of the parish.

01

Liturgical by design

Campaigns follow the Orthodox calendar — Nativity, Theophany, Great Lent, Pascha, Soul Saturdays, patronal feasts — with both Julian and Gregorian calendars supported.

02

Commemorations reach the priest

Memorial offerings are formatted and routed toward the priest’s preparation, so names are not lost in a spreadsheet.

03

Canonical standing is reviewed

Every parish and monastery is reviewed before activation, so gifts reach recognized Orthodox communities.

04

Monasteries are honored

Monasteries and sketes carry no monthly platform fee — permanently, by conviction. Standard transaction fees still apply.

05

No long-term lock-ins

No annual contracts. No cancellation penalties. A platform should earn a parish’s trust each month.
