Building the Online home for all things Orthodox.

AGAPAY exists to strengthen Orthodox communities through modern financial infrastructure rooted in shared faith, stewardship, and long-term institutional support.

We believe Orthodox churches, ministries, schools, businesses, monasteries, and faithful families deserve tools built specifically for the life of the Church.

Why AGAPAY Exists

Orthodox communities deserve infrastructure built for them.

AGAPAY was created to build a connected financial ecosystem that helps Orthodox institutions grow stronger together.

The Challenge

Orthodox institutions are digitally fragmented.

Financial activity leaves the ecosystem. Resources become scattered. Communities remain disconnected.

Our Response

A unified ecosystem built on faith, trust, and stewardship.

AGAPAY creates modern infrastructure to help Orthodox life become more connected, sustainable, and resilient.

The Three-Phase Vision
1
Phase One

Orthodox Giving Infrastructure

The first phase focuses on building modern software for Orthodox churches and parishes.

  • Digital giving and stewardship tools
  • Recurring donations
  • Parish fundraising and event payments
  • Financial management integrations
  • Donor engagement tools
2
Phase Two

The Orthodox Marketplace

The second phase expands into an Orthodox marketplace where businesses, monasteries, and creators connect directly with the faithful.

  • Orthodox businesses and monastery goods
  • Books, icons, and sacred art
  • Vestments and church supplies
  • Handcrafted and digital products
  • Events and pilgrimage services
3
Phase Three

Ministries, Nonprofits, & Schools

The third phase extends infrastructure support for ministries, nonprofits, schools, and mission-driven Orthodox organizations.

  • Fundraising and donor management
  • Tuition and payment systems
  • Grant and sponsorship support
  • Event registration and engagement
  • Mission support and giving networks
