Our Vision

THE HOME

For all things Orthodox.

AGAPAY exists to strengthen Orthodox communities through modern giving infrastructure rooted in shared faith, stewardship, and long-term institutional support. We believe Orthodox parishes, monasteries, and people deserve tools built specifically for the life of the Church, with a vision that grows to serve the wider Orthodox world.

Why AGAPAY Exists

Orthodox communities deserve infrastructure built for them.

AGAPAY was created to build a connected giving platform that helps Orthodox institutions grow stronger together.

The Challenge

Orthodox institutions are digitally fragmented.

Financial activity leaves the community. Resources become scattered. Communities remain disconnected.

Our Response

A unified platform built on faith, trust, and stewardship.

AGAPAY creates modern infrastructure to help Orthodox life become more connected, sustainable, and resilient.
