Why AGAPAY Give?

AGAPAY Give was born from a simple conviction: Orthodox Christians deserve an online giving platform built by Orthodox Christians, for Orthodox Christians. While most giving platforms were designed for generic nonprofits or megachurches, AGAPAY Give was created from within the life of the Church, with a deep appreciation for Orthodox worship, stewardship, and parish life.

AGAPAY Give transforms online giving from a mere transaction into a more meaningful connection between the faithful and their parish. Donors can support stewardship, candles, memorials, commemorations, special collections, and parish campaigns through a platform designed around the rhythms and traditions of Orthodox life.

For parishes, missions, and monasteries, AGAPAY Give provides modern tools to increase participation, simplify administration, and strengthen financial stewardship without compromising Orthodox values. By bringing giving, commemorations, donor engagement, and parish support together in one purpose-built platform, AGAPAY helps communities focus less on managing technology and more on the work of the Gospel.

Because giving is more than a payment. It is an act of love, gratitude, and participation in the life of the Church.
