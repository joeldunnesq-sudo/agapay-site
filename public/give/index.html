THE PROBLEM

Our communities deserve better giving infrastructure.

For too long, Orthodox parishes and monasteries have relied on disconnected giving platforms built without their communities in mind.

High fees, fragmented tools, and value-neutral systems weaken long-term stewardship and pull resources away from the institutions that sustain Orthodox life.

AGAPAY was created to provide a modern, trusted giving platform designed specifically for Orthodox communities.
