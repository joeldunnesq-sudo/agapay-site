Common Questions

Orthodox giving, made clearer.

AGAPAY Give is purpose-built for the practical and spiritual rhythms of Orthodox generosity.

What is an Orthodox giving app?

An Orthodox giving app is online giving software designed around Orthodox parish life. AGAPAY Give supports stewardship, tithes, candles, memorials, commemorations, campaigns, and donor records in one platform.

Can parishioners make recurring tithes and donations?

Yes. Donors can make one-time or recurring gifts to verified Orthodox parishes and monasteries through secure Stripe checkout.

Can donors submit candles, memorials, and names for commemoration?

Yes. AGAPAY Give supports candle offerings, memorial gifts, and names of the living and departed for parish commemoration workflows.

Is Text-to-Give available?

Text-to-Give is a coming-soon AGAPAY Give feature. The current platform supports web giving, parish campaigns, recurring gifts, QR codes, candles, memorials, commemorations, and donor records.
